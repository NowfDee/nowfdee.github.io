🔍 Understanding The Process

This application links four specific tools to build your video:

  • Ollama acts as the brain, processing your text to create structured content.
  • Edge-TTS turns your text into a natural sounding human voice.
  • Pexels provides high-quality stock imagery and clips for your visuals.
  • FFmpeg stitches all these parts together into a finished video file.

💡 Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues during the process, follow these tips:

  • Check your internet connection if the app fails to fetch video clips.
  • Ensure you have enough disk space if the rendering process stops mid-way.
  • Restart the application if the progress bar stays stuck for more than five minutes.
  • Verify that your antivirus software is not blocking the connection to the local web interface.
